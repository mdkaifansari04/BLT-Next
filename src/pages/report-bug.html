Help make the internet more secure by reporting bugs and vulnerabilities.

Reporting Tips

  • Be specific — include the exact URL or page where the bug appears.
  • Include clear steps to reproduce so maintainers can verify the issue.
  • Attach screenshots or screen recordings for visual bugs.
  • For security vulnerabilities, describe potential impact and attack vector.

Severity Guide

  • Critical System compromise, data breach
  • High Significant data exposure
  • Medium Limited impact, partial access
  • Low Minimal impact on users
  • Info Best practice suggestion

Earn Rewards

Valid bug reports earn points, badges, and may qualify for monetary rewards from bug bounty programs.
